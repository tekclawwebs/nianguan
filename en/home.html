​Corporate Health Management

​Current State of Employee Health Management

On November 4, 2019, the National Health Commission and other agencies issued the "Standard for the Construction of Health Enterprises (Trial)," which encourages enterprises to establish medical rooms or emergency response stations according to specified standards. These facilities provide medical and emergency services as well as health management. 

The guidelines also suggest that the construction of health enterprises can involve outsourcing to professional organizations.
